App Purpose & Vision

SPHERIX bridges the gap between citizens and urban planning by leveraging NASA Earth observation data, municipal information, and community input to create a responsive, data-driven platform for urban resilience.

Mission Statement

To empower residents as active participants in urban sustainability through personalized insights, actionable recommendations, and streamlined civic engagement tools.

Core Features

Comprehensive functionality for citizen engagement and urban resilience

1
Onboarding & Privacy Consent

Simple welcome flow introducing the app purpose with clear privacy controls and data usage transparency.

2
Data Policy Transparency

Clear explanation of data sources (NASA, municipal, citizen) and purpose-based usage breakdown.

3
Home / Local Overview

Interactive city map with environmental risk badges (Heat, Air, Population, Green Spaces) and layered data visualization.

4
Action Recommendations

Personalized daily/weekly tasks with source labels, confidence scores, and completion tracking.

5
Alerts & Push Notifications

Real-time emergency advisories (heat warnings, air quality drops) with safety tips and authority contacts.

6
Issue Reporting

One-tap submission for urban problems with photo upload, auto-location, and municipal routing.

7
Volunteer & Community

Event listings for sustainability drives with participation tracking and social features.

8
My Impact Dashboard

Personal progress tracking with gamified badges, contribution metrics, and community leaderboards.

Urban Planning Impact

For Citizens

  • Personalized environmental insights and health recommendations
  • Streamlined civic engagement and issue reporting
  • Community connection through shared sustainability goals
  • Transparent understanding of urban data and its applications
  • Empowerment through measurable impact tracking

For Urban Planners

  • Real-time ground truth data from citizen reports
  • Enhanced understanding of local environmental concerns
  • Data-driven prioritization of urban interventions
  • Increased community buy-in for sustainability initiatives
  • Comprehensive monitoring of urban health indicators

Integrated Data Ecosystem

SPHERIX creates a feedback loop where NASA satellite data informs citizen recommendations, while citizen reports ground-truth and enhance the satellite observations, creating a continuously improving understanding of urban environments.

NASA Data → Citizen Insights → Urban Planning
